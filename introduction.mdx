## what's sync?

sync is a research company developing cutting-edge ai video solutions, currently focusing on lipsyncing models.

our api makes it simple to sync lip movements in any video to match audio input, whether you're working on movies, podcasts, games, or animations.

designed for easy integration, our api scales seamlessly with your apps, platforms, or services as your user base grows.
